## About Me

I hold a Master's degree in Control Science and Engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology (2018-2021) and a Bachelor's degree in Automation from Nanjing Agricultural University (2014-2018). Currently, I work as a Senior Algorithm Engineer at Hangzhou iMedAI, where I focus on developing LLM-based academic writing agents, RAG systems, and AI-powered platforms for traditional Chinese medicine.

My research background includes cloud-based teleoperated vascular interventional robotic systems, ECG signal analysis, and intelligent control systems. I have multiple publications in IEEE Transactions on Robotics, Physiological Measurement, and other reputable journals, as well as several patents in medical AI and robotics.
